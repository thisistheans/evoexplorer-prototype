Predict: Pink Salmon

Predict the changes you would expect to see in the pink salmon population in 34 years with warmer climate.

  • There will be an increase in early migrators because early migrators are able to survive and reproduce and a decrease of late-migrators since they won’t be able to survive.
  • There will be a decrease in early migrators because early migrators will not be able to survive and reproduce as efficiently as the late migrators.
  • There will be no significant change in the proportion of early vs late migrators because there haven’t been enough generations to allow for selection to occur.
  • More information is needed to answer this question.
